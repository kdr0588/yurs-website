President

The President is the Chief Executive Officer of YURS and is the most powerful position on the Executive Council. The President represents YURS and shall be responsible for establishing and preserving contacts between YURS and other organizations. The President presides at all meetings of the members of the organization, as well as the Executive Council. The President shall see to the general and active management of the affairs of the organization. The President is also responsible for ensuring that the rules and bylaws of YURS are carried into effect. The President has the power to interpret the Constitution as written. The President is a signing officer, with the power to approve or reject the proposed budget.

Treasurer

The Treasurer maintains the funds of YURS and creates accounts of expenditures and revenues. The Treasurer is responsible for ensuring our bank account is maintained and usable. The Treasurer is in charge of reimbursements (together with the President). The Treasurer also has a role in creating the budget for YURS. The Treasurer is a signing officer of YURS.

Vice President, Events and Outreach

The VPEO would attend to the planning and scheduling of events for YURS. YURS has a constitutional objective of outreach, and to that end, the VPEO can schedule workshops, tours, school visits, and various other activities as deemed necessary to that end. The VPEO shall be responsible for establishing, maintaining, and preserving contacts between YURS and such organizations as may be contacted from time to time for the purpose of events or outreach activities. Coming off a year where little outreach was possible, the VPEO will have a chance to make a big impact as we hopefully return to a "normal" school year. This position is a full executive position, which means that there is a considerable role in club affairs, though it does not have signing authority.

Mechanical Team

The mechanical team designs and builds our rovers that are used in competitions. If you prefer to get hands-on, then this is the sub-team for you! Designed in Fusion 360, examples of parts that the mechanical team has designed includes a cartesian/SCARA hybrid arm for the equipment servicing task, and a simplified rocker-bogie system for terrain traversal!
